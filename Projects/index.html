Welcome to the Hydra Project

The "Hydra Project" is a mutli-platform application for an order management system of a company that provides inspection services for vessels across Europe. A powerful tool that provides clear, correct and accessible information about the workflow of the business to everyone involved.

The problem with the whiteboard

Prior to "Hydra Project", the order system of the company was based on a handwritten whiteboard, only accessible from the office. When the workflow was heavy and technicians were on the road, it was easy for them to lose control of the next assignments as they are dynamic: Dates of arrival for vessel changes all the time, ports of berthing can change, more data always coming about necessary repairs etc.

"Employees didn't have access to valuable information, making room for errors, misunderstandings, and waste of time."

  • Jonathon Andrew , SG Euro

Increase productivity with "Project Hydra"

With the transition to a secure and modern multi-platform application with an efficient and well-designed database, the company and the employees have always access to the workflow from wherever they are thanks to the ASP .Net web application and the mobile app

"It's easy to add, edit and control all orders in real time and keep the workflow in order."

  • Jonathon Andrew , SG Euro
